Courses you need to learn as software engineer by the end of this year

Artificial intelligence and machine learning

Covers topics like neural networks, deep learning, natural language processing, and AI ethics. AI and ML are transforming industries from healthcare to finance, making this skill set highly valuable.

Blockchain technology and cryptography

Learn about blockchain technology, smart contracts, and decentralized applications (dApps). With increasing adoption of cryptocurrencies and blockchain-based solutions, understanding this technology is crucial.

Extended reality: Virtual reality(VR) and Augmented reality(AR)

Learn to create immersive experiences using VR/AR technologies. XR is rapidly gaining traction in entertainment, education, and training

Data science and big data analytics

Focus on data analysis techniques, data visualization, and predictive modeling. As organizations increasingly rely on data-driven decisions, expertise in data science and big data is highly sought after.

Natural Language Processing

Delve into the techniques used for text processing, sentiment analysis, and chatbot development. NLP is essential + for AI-driven applications like virtual assistants, automated customer service, and content analysis

Biotechnology and Bioinformatics

Study the intersection of biology and technology, including gene editing (CRISPR), computational biology, and health informatics. This field is gaining importance in personalized medicine, drug development, and genetic research
